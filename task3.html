The World

The world is created in Unity for a game called VRChat.
The world includes two types of toggleable mirrors. A low quality mirror that only mirrors the players and the skybox
and a high quality mirror that mirrors everything.
I used C# to code the world.
There is a radio that is toggleable in the world aswell that plays some background music.

The Avatar

The avatar I created using Blender and Unity.
The avatar had dynamic bone physics so the hair moves in a realistic way.
On the fingertips of the avatar it has colliders so you can use them to move the hair and other parts that have dynamic bones.
